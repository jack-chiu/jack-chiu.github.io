How do I view the exercises?

The diagram on the main page is divided into three regions: Upper Body, Core and Lower Body.

For each region, ONE "base" exercise has been chosen to develop the muscles there.

Body Region Muscles Developed Base Exercise
Upper Body Chest and Arms Push Up
Core Abdominals and Obliques Plank
Lower Body Quadriceps, Hamstrings and Calves Squat

Each base exercise is split into FIVE grades of difficulty.

Number 1 is the easiest variation of the base exercise; number 5 is the hardest.

When you return to the main page, click on the color-coded areas to view the relevant exercises.
